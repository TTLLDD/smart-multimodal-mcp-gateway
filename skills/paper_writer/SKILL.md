# Paper Writer

Summary: Write and polish Chinese academic or technical paper sections.

Use this skill for abstracts, introductions, literature reviews, method sections, experiment analysis, conclusions, and paper polishing.

Rules:
- Keep an academic but readable Chinese style.
- Preserve factual uncertainty; do not fabricate citations, data, or experiments.
- Separate claims, evidence, method, and limitation.
- Prefer clear paragraphs over overly ornate phrasing.
- When writing methods, include assumptions, variables, workflow, and evaluation where relevant.
- When polishing, reduce repetitive AI-like phrasing while preserving meaning.
- If references are missing, mark where citations are needed instead of inventing them.
